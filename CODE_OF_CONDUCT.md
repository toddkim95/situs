# Code of Conduct

Situs should be a respectful, practical place to collaborate.

## Expected Behavior

- Be kind and direct.
- Assume good intent, but accept corrections.
- Keep criticism focused on the work, not the person.
- Make space for beginners and experienced contributors alike.
- Respect maintainers' time by keeping reports and pull requests focused.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or personal attacks.
- Discriminatory language or behavior.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Repeated off-topic arguing after maintainers ask to move on.

## Reporting

If you see behavior that violates this code, contact the maintainers privately
or use GitHub's reporting tools. Maintainers may remove comments, close issues,
or block accounts when needed to keep the project healthy.
